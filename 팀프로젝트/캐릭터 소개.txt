          <h1>Bo Peep</h1>
          <span>
            Bo Peep is a long-lost friend of Woody, Buzz and the gang, who
            always shared a special connection with Woody while residing with
            her sheep on a lamp base at Andy’s house.
            <br />After being separated for years, Bo has become chipped and
            discarded, but her spirit is far from broken. <br />She has grown
            into an adventure-seeking free spirit whose strength and sarcasm
            belie her delicate porcelain exterior. <br />When she and Woody are
            reunited under unlikely circumstances, Bo realizes just how much
            she’s missed him, and can’t wait to show him what she’s been up to.
